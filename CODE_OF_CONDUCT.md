# Code of Conduct

Standards:
- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
- Focus on constructive feedback and collaboration
- Welcome people of all backgrounds and experience levels

Example of unacceptable behaviours:
- Personal attacks or insults
- Using sexualized language
- Discriminatory comments or behavior
- Harassment of any kind
- Publishing other's private info without any permissions
- Any other unehtical/professional conduct

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please let me know.

I will maintain a positive environment where everyone feels safe to contribute. I have the right to remove/edit any comments/commits/code/issues and any other contributions that do not follow this Code of Conduct.
